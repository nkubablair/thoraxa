ELECTRIC VEHICLE PROJECT VALUES.


MISSION:Creating a new standard for electric vehicles ThoraxaMotors is dedicated to changing Africa's view of electric cars. THORAXA, which is based in KABALE, UGANDA, was founded in 2020 by NKUBA BLAIR who set out to prove that electric vehicles did not have to be unattractive, slow econoboxes with limited range. Instead, they could be awesome. The company proved its point with its very first model, THE THORAXA MODEL 1, It is arguably one of the most attractive sports cars on the road and boasts performance that few expected from an electric horsepower, a top speed of 125 miles per hour, and 0-to-60 acceleration in less than four seconds. The THORAXA is now sold in more than 30 countries Afrcawide. Thoraxa's mission is to make electricity a viable alternative to petroleum fuel in vehicles. It is already succeeding in making that case with its own electric cars and is also supplying electric Electric vehicle power to partner manufacturers including Kiira EV.
